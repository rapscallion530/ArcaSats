Reconciliation inbox

When coins leave one of your wallets and reappear in another through an address ArcaSats doesn't track, the two on-chain transactions have different txids — so they can't be linked automatically. These are suggestions, never auto-applied: confirm a pair to relabel both as a self-transfer and carry the cost basis across, or reject it if they're genuinely separate buy/sell events. Exact shared-txid transfers are reconciled automatically and don't appear here.
